# the big idea of economics

- **what's in a name** $\rightarrow$ economics comes from a Greek word meaning "managing a household" it is just managing resources but on a huge scale
- **Definition** $\rightarrow$ economics is the study of how people and societies decide to use their limited resources to make and the stuff they want
