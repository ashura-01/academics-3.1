### Processor Bus

processor bus also known as front side bus (FSB) and back side bus (BSB)
**characteristics:**
- highest speed bus in the system
- used by processor to communicate with
	- cache  (BSB)
	- Main memory (FSB)
	- North Bridge
runs at 800 MHz or higher and is normally 64 bits wide

### AGP - Accelerate Graphics Port

special video cards which have been designed with extra onboard RAM a specialized graphics graphics processor and other features. It is useless unless they have high speed and system RAM access

connected directly to north bridge/GHCH. AGP is a high speed bus with clock speed of 66.66 MHz
